Our Values


MISSION: Our mission is to be the voice of the people of West Village C and West Village F and to provide fun and engaging events for everyone. We plan to represent our constituency with the utmost vigilance and accuracy, consistently reaching out to the people of "the neighborhood" to determine the general opinions on what needs improvement.  We plan to use this website and the West Village C/F Facebook page as an open forum for YOUR ideas and suggestions on how to improve our community.  Many people have already expressed to us a desire to have a printer moved into the lobby of West Village F.  If elected, that will be our first order of business.


VISION: We envision a politically active and engaged community that is run by a caring group of your peers.  West Village C and West Village F will be transformed by social, culinary, and entertaining events that benefit all.



About Us





The four of us live in West Village F.  Come down to the 3rd floor anytime, we always keep the door open while we are cooking Frank’s World Famous Burgers or playing some FIFA. In our free time we can often be found playing basketball at Marino or exploring the great city of Boston.




Bios


Jude
President

Jude is a second year Political Science major from Long Island. On campus he is actively involved in the International Relations Council and is a realstate agent for students moving off campus. He is running for President of West Village C and West Village F because he believes that he will be able to appease the community by listening to the opinions of fellow West Villagers and implementing policy that reflects their desires. He hopes that you will cast your vote for him on voting day because he truly wants to be a positive influence in our neighborhood.


Manas
Vice President

Manas is a second year Computer Science & Business major from Boston. He fixes students’ computers as a hardware technician at ResNet and tutors freshmen in a first-year computer science class. He believes he will be able to use his technical and communication skills to serve and aid the community. In his free time, he likes to go camping with NUHOC, make apps, and play basketball.
